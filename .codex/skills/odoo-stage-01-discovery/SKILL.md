# Odoo Stage 01 Discovery

## Overview
Produce a structured discovery report that describes the current process, key pain points, business objectives, and preliminary scope boundaries for an Odoo custom module initiative.
Use `odoo-19` as a global technical knowledge source only when technical details are needed.

## Required Inputs
- Business objective and target process area.
- Main stakeholders and operating roles.
- Current workflow description (AS-IS) and known pain points.
- Constraints (timeline, budget, compliance, operational limits).

## Workflow
1. Capture the AS-IS process with role-by-role activity mapping.
2. Identify measurable pain points, root causes, and business impact.
3. Define high-level TO-BE intent without prescribing technical solutions.
4. Mark preliminary scope boundaries (in-scope, out-of-scope, unknown).
5. Record open questions and decision owners.

## Outputs
- Discovery report with AS-IS process map and pain-point matrix.
- Preliminary scope statement with explicit assumptions.
- Stakeholder and decision log for the next stage.

## Definition of Done
- Current process and business problem are traceable and explicit.
- Pain points are prioritized by impact and frequency.
- Scope boundaries are clear enough to start functional definition.

## Handoff
Provide the stage output to `$odoo-stage-02-functional-definition` including:
- Validated business objectives and scope boundaries.
- Prioritized pain points and unresolved questions.
- Stakeholder approvals or pending decisions.
